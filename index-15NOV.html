Vitamin A

Benefits

  • Protects Your Eyes From Night Blindness and Age-Related Decline.
  • May Lower Your Risk of Certain Cancers.
  • Supports a Healthy Immune System

Symptoms

  • Dry Skin
  • Dry Eyes
  • Night Blindness
  • Delayed Growth

Vitamin B

Benefits

  • Reduces inflammation, dryness, and acne.
  • Vital role in maintaining good health and well-being.
  • Help some signs of skin aging.

Symptoms

  • Tiredness and weakness
  • Constipation
  • Loss of appetite
  • Weight loss
